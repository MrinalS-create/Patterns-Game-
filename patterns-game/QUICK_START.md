# 🎮 Quick Start Guide - The Patterns Journey

## 🚀 How to Play

### Opening the Game
1. Navigate to: `/Users/ms/Downloads/Patterns/patterns-game/`
2. Double-click `index.html`
3. Click "Start Level" when ready

## 🎯 Your Mission

**Objective**: Complete World 1-1: Foundation Valley

### What to Do:
1. ✅ **Collect the EDT Lens** (hexagon tool with "T" on it)
2. ✅ **Defeat 3 Confusion Fogs** (purple cloudy enemies with "?")
3. ✅ **Collect Evidence** (yellow stars) - optional but gives points
4. ✅ **Reach the Exit** (green door with 🚪)

## 🕹️ Controls

### Movement
- **← →** or **A D** - Move left/right
- **Space** - Jump
- **Space (in air)** - Double jump

### Tools
- **1-9** - Activate tools (after collecting)
- **Escape** - Pause game
- **F** - Debug mode (see collision boxes)

## 📍 Finding Items

### EDT Lens Tool (Hexagon)
- **Location**: On the second floating platform
- **How to get**: Jump up the platforms to reach it
- **What it does**: When collected, appears in your tool inventory (top right)
- **How to use**: Press **1** to activate (costs energy)
- **Effect**: Reveals hidden paths and enemy weak points

### Evidence (Yellow Stars)
- **Location**: Scattered across the ground level
- **How to get**: Walk over them
- **What they do**: Give you points and track progress
- **Count**: 5 total in the level

### Enemies (Purple Fogs)
- **Location**: Patrolling on platforms
- **How to defeat**: Jump on them from above (like Mario!)
- **Warning**: Don't touch them from the side - you'll take damage!
- **Reward**: +50 points per enemy

### Exit Door (Green)
- **Location**: Far right side of the level
- **How to reach**: Navigate all platforms to get there
- **What happens**: Completes the level and shows your stats

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Combat
- ✅ **DO**: Jump on enemies from above
- ❌ **DON'T**: Touch enemies from the side
- 💡 **TIP**: Use double jump to reach higher platforms safely

### Collection
- The **EDT Lens** is on a platform - you need to jump to reach it
- **Evidence stars** are on the ground - just walk over them
- **Health pickups** (green hearts) restore your health

### Navigation
- Use **double jump** to reach high platforms
- The **camera follows you** - explore freely
- **Brown platforms** are solid - you can stand on them
- **Blue character "A"** is you (Alex)

## 📊 Understanding the UI

### Top Left - Energy Bar
- Shows your current energy (30/100)
- Used when activating tools
- Regenerates slowly over time

### Top Right - Tool Inventory
- 9 slots for tools (numbered 1-9)
- Collected tools appear here
- Press the number to activate
- Cooldown timer shows when tool is recharging

### Bottom Right - Framework Mastery
- Shows progress in 6 frameworks:
  - EDT (End of Thinking)
  - PDLC (Product Development Lifecycle)
  - Team (Team Dynamics)
  - Communication
  - Execution
  - Impact
- Increases as you complete levels
- **Now semi-transparent** so it doesn't block view!

### Bottom Right - Level Info
- Current level name
- Time elapsed

## 🎓 What Happens When You Collect Things

### Collecting EDT Lens:
1. **Notification appears**: "Collected: EDT Lens! Press 1-9 to use"
2. **Tool appears** in inventory slot 1 (top right)
3. **Tutorial hint**: "Great! You collected the EDT Lens. Press 1 to use it!"
4. **You can now**: Press **1** to activate the tool

### Collecting Evidence:
1. **Notification appears**: "Evidence: 1/5" (shows progress)
2. **Points added** to your score
3. **Star disappears** from the level

### Defeating Enemy:
1. **Notification appears**: "Enemy Defeated! +50 points"
2. **Tutorial hint**: "Nice! Keep jumping on enemies!"
3. **Enemy disappears**
4. **Score increases**

### Taking Damage:
1. **Notification appears**: "Took 10 damage!"
2. **Tutorial hint**: "Watch out! Avoid touching enemies from the side!"
3. **Health bar decreases**
4. **You become invincible** for 1.5 seconds (flashing)

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "I can't see the EDT Lens!"
- It's on the **second floating platform** (brown rectangle)
- Jump up from the ground to reach it
- Look for a **hexagon shape** with "T" on it
- It has a **label "EDT Lens"** underneath

### "Framework Mastery is blocking my view!"
- It's now **semi-transparent** (70% opacity)
- Hover over it to see it fully
- It's moved to **bottom right** corner
- Made **smaller and more compact**

### "What's the timeline?"
- The **timer** shows how long you've been playing
- It's just for tracking - no time limit!
- Appears in bottom right: "Time: 3:17"

### "I took damage and don't know why!"
- You touched an enemy from the **side or front**
- Only jump on them from **above** to defeat them
- You get **invincibility frames** after taking damage (flashing)

### "How do I use the EDT Lens?"
1. Collect it first (walk over the hexagon)
2. Check top right - it should appear in slot 1
3. Press **1** on your keyboard
4. You'll see: "EDT Lens activated!"
5. It costs **20 energy** to use

## 🎯 Step-by-Step Walkthrough

1. **Start**: Click "Start Level"
2. **Move Right**: Press → or D
3. **Jump**: Press Space to jump onto first platform
4. **Double Jump**: Press Space again in air to reach higher
5. **Collect EDT Lens**: On second platform (hexagon)
6. **Defeat Enemies**: Jump on the 3 purple fogs
7. **Collect Stars**: Walk over yellow stars on ground
8. **Reach Exit**: Navigate to the green door on far right
9. **Complete**: Walk into the door to finish!
